Pumpkin Patch

Fall @ Ardenwood Farms

Ardenwood Farms opens their Pumpkin Patch each year for the public to enjoy the fall aesthetic. Here, we went right before golden hour to capture the warm tones of the vibrant farm as the sun sets in the background. In post-editing, I accentuated the bright colors and lowered saturation of the sky & background.

San Francisco

Spring @ Market Street

San Francisco is a rich, vibrant city filled with so much life and beauty, and yet I chose to take these pictures at a Pete's Coffee on Market Street! In post-editing I highlighted the warmth and richness of the chocolate brown, wooden background and brought out the violet by increasing saturation for the color.

Burlingame

Winter @ Burlingame Downtown

These photos were taken at a local train station towards the end of a day-trip to Burlingame, CA. I accentuated the rich blues, deep nudes, and bright oranges in post-editing, and increased the brightness of the grays to appear like bright steel, all while reducing the noise of the background to make the images look cloud-like.
