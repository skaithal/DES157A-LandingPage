Rules

  • Roll the dice when it is your turn. Then number you roll gets added to your score.
  • If you roll a one, the turn passes to the next player.
  • You can also choose to pass your turn to the next player.
  • Be careful! If you roll two ones, or ‘Snake Eyes,’ your score gets set back to 0.
  • The first player to reach a score of 30 wins!
